Burger 336

Basic Info

Location:

1400 Spring Garden Street Greensboro, NC 27412

Inside of the food court

Hours:

Monday - Thursday: 10:30AM - 11:00PM

Friday: 10:30AM - 3:00PM

Saturday: CLOSED

Sunday: 6:00PM - 10:00PM

How To Order:

In-Person or through Transact Mobile Ordering

How to Pay:

Flex, Card, or Cash

Current Specials:

All Beef Hotdog with Fries and a Fountian Drink for $9.99

Review

This is Burger 336 a burger place in the food court of UNCG's campus. They sell enjoyable burgers with amazing fries. They are very inexpensive and there is a multitude of ways to pay.

Recommendations

My first recommendation is the Spartan Burger. The Spartan Burger is my go-to meal with all the toppings that make a burger worth paying for. With a drink and side of fries this meal is sure to fill you up.

My second recommendation is the Walker Avenue Black Bean Burger. I'm not one who would go out of their way to eat beans, but this burger is the exception. With awesome toppings and amazing black bean patty, this burger is sure to make your taste buds melt.

My third recommendation is the McIver Bacon Double Burger. Named after the founder of the university this burger is what I would consider a monster burger. With bacon and 2 patties this burger is sure to leave you stuffed.

Panda Express

Basic Info
Location:

1209 North Dr

Greensboro, NC 27412

Inside of the food court

Hours:

Monday - Thursday: 11:00AM - 9:00PM

Friday: 10:30AM - 7:00PM

Saturday: CLOSED

Sunday: 5:00PM - 10:00PM

How To Order:

In-Person or through Transact Mobile Ordering

How to Pay

Flex, Card, or Cash

Things To Know

You can only get either a bowl(1 entree), plate(2 entrees) or bigger plate(3 entrees) at Panda Express that is why I am just recommending the entrees.

The bowl has more food (or at least it feels that way).

The cups are to small so I would go up a size

Review

Panda Express is a well know fast-food chain in the United States. Selling yummy entrees and sides that can be mixed and matched, you can have a multitude of different dishes every time you order. The Panda Express is a must while being on UNCG's campus.

Recommendations

My first recommendation for Panda Express would have to be the Grilled Teriyaki Chicken. One of my favorite sauces on chicken would have to be teriyaki sauce and this entree is no exception. The grilled teriyaki is my second favorite entree. The first is to the right.

My second recommendation is the Orange Chicken. This is the most well know product on the menu and it does not disappoint. When you have this orange chicken you will understand why it is everyone's number one choice. including mine!

My third recommendation is the Honey Walnut Shrimp. I know what you are thinking could the shrimp be good. Well, no it is not good, it is scrumptious. It is one of my new favorites and one I hope you try. The only thing is that it cost $1.25 more on top of anything you order.


Chick-Fl-A

Basic Info
Location:

1400 Spring Garden Street

Greensboro, NC 27412

First floor of the Elliot University Center

Hours:

Monday - Thursday: 7:30AM - 10:30PM

Friday: 7:30AM - 8:00PM

Sunday: CLOSED

How To Order:

In-Person or through Transact Mobile Ordering

How to Pay

Flex, Card, or Cash

Things To Know

The milkshakes take a very long time to make

Review
Recommendations

Chick-Fl-A is a well-known fast-food chain in the United States. With long lines almost every time you go you know it is a must have whenever traveling. Even when coming to UNCG's campus.

My first recommendation is the Spicy Chicken Sandwich. This spicy spin on the original is sure to make you sweat. When added with mayonnaise it is sure to get your mouth watering. And if you don't like spice the original is always an option.

My second recommendation is the 12 count chicken nuggets. This is my go-to when I do not know what I want. My favorite sauce to pair it with is Chick-Fl-A sauce. And even though an 8 count is the same you just feel fuller with a 12 count.

My third recommendation is the Cobb salad. You may be saying isn't ordering salad at a fast-food restaurant like eating fish at a steak house? And my answer to that question is yes, but Chick-Fl-A is the exception. I have never had a gross salad from Chick-Fl-A and hopefully I never will. I have recommended the Cobb salad because it is my favorite, but truly all the salads are delicious.
